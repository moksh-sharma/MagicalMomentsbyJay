Design a premium, luxury photography portfolio website for the brand “Magical Moments by Jai” by Jai Ahuja.

Business type: Indian wedding photography, pre-wedding photoshoots, cinematic wedding films, couple shoots, engagement shoots, and full wedding event coverage.

Instagram reference/inspiration: https://www.instagram.com/magical_moments_by_jai_ahuja/

Create a complete modern website in a black and golden luxury theme. The website should feel elegant, cinematic, emotional, royal, and premium — perfect for Indian weddings. Use a dark black background with rich gold accents, soft gradients, warm lighting effects, elegant serif typography for headings, and clean modern sans-serif typography for body text.

The design should look like a high-end wedding photography brand website, not a normal portfolio.

Website pages/sections required:

1. Hero Section
- Full-screen cinematic hero with luxury Indian wedding couple image/video background.
- Brand name: “Magical Moments by Jai”
- Tagline: “Capturing Love, Rituals & Timeless Memories”
- Subtext: “Premium Wedding Photography & Pre-Wedding Shoots”
- CTA buttons:
  - “View Portfolio”
  - “Book Your Shoot”
- Add small gold decorative elements inspired by Indian weddings: mandala patterns, subtle floral borders, diya glow, or royal frame lines.
- Include Instagram icon and WhatsApp contact button.

2. About Section
- Title: “Behind the Lens”
- Introduce Jai as a passionate wedding photographer who captures emotions, rituals, candid moments, and cinematic stories.
- Layout: one elegant portrait/image area on one side and text on the other.
- Include 3 premium highlights:
  - Candid Wedding Photography
  - Cinematic Storytelling
  - Luxury Pre-Wedding Shoots

3. Wedding Moments Section
Create a beautiful section showing different Indian wedding functions as cards or horizontal scroll gallery:
- Roka Ceremony
- Engagement
- Mehendi
- Haldi
- Sangeet
- Wedding Day
- Reception
- Bridal Portraits
- Groom Portraits
Each card should have image/video thumbnail, gold title overlay, hover zoom effect, and soft shadow.

4. Featured Portfolio Gallery
- Masonry-style gallery with premium wedding photos and videos.
- Categories/tabs:
  - All
  - Wedding
  - Pre-Wedding
  - Haldi
  - Mehendi
  - Sangeet
  - Bridal
  - Reception
- Use mixed image sizes for a cinematic look.
- Add hover overlay: “View Story”
- Add lightbox preview interaction concept.

5. Cinematic Wedding Films Section
- Dark cinematic section with large video preview cards.
- Add play button overlay in gold.
- Heading: “Wedding Films That Feel Like Cinema”
- Include 3 video cards:
  - Wedding Teaser
  - Pre-Wedding Film
  - Full Wedding Highlights

6. Services Section
Create premium service cards:
- Wedding Photography
- Pre-Wedding Photoshoot
- Cinematic Wedding Films
- Engagement Photography
- Traditional Photography
- Candid Photography
- Drone Shoots
- Albums & Wedding Books
Each card should include icon, short description, and “Enquire Now” button.

7. Pre-Wedding Experience Section
- Romantic section focused on outdoor/location-based couple shoots.
- Include content around concepts, styling, locations, cinematic poses, and storytelling.
- Add CTA: “Plan Your Dream Pre-Wedding Shoot”

8. Why Choose Us Section
Use premium numbered points:
- Emotion-first storytelling
- Candid + traditional coverage
- Cinematic color grading
- High-quality edited photos and films
- Personalized shoot concepts
- Professional wedding workflow
- Fast delivery and premium albums

9. Packages Section
Create 3 luxury pricing/package cards without exact prices:
- Essential Moments
- Royal Wedding Story
- Luxury Cinematic Experience
Each card should show inclusions like photography, videography, drone, album, teaser, reels, full film, etc.
Add button: “Get Custom Quote”

10. Testimonials Section
- Elegant carousel-style testimonials from couples.
- Use couple names as placeholders.
- Include star rating in gold.
- Add emotional review text.

11. Instagram Showcase Section
- Heading: “Follow Our Latest Stories”
- Show a grid inspired by Instagram posts/reels.
- Include CTA: “View on Instagram”

12. Contact / Booking Section
- Premium enquiry form:
  - Name
  - Phone / WhatsApp
  - Email
  - Event Type
  - Event Date
  - Location
  - Message
- Add CTA button: “Send Enquiry”
- Also add quick contact buttons:
  - WhatsApp
  - Call Now
  - Instagram DM
- Add text: “Let’s capture your magical moments.”

13. Footer
- Brand name: Magical Moments by Jai
- Quick links
- Services
- Instagram link
- WhatsApp/contact area
- Copyright line

Design style:
- Theme: black, charcoal, deep brown-black, metallic gold, champagne gold.
- Typography: luxury serif heading font, modern clean body font.
- Layout: spacious, premium, cinematic.
- Use rounded cards, glassmorphism panels, soft golden borders, subtle glow effects.
- Add Indian wedding-inspired design elements: mandala patterns, floral motifs, royal borders, soft bokeh lights, golden particles, warm lens flare.
- Use high-quality placeholder images of Indian weddings, bridal portraits, groom portraits, haldi, mehendi, sangeet, pre-wedding shoots, and wedding rituals.
- Include both photo and video placeholders.

Interactions and animations:
- Smooth scrolling experience.
- Hero text fade-in.
- Gallery hover zoom.
- Gold underline animation on navigation.
- Video play overlay animation.
- Cards reveal on scroll.
- Floating WhatsApp button.
- Sticky elegant navigation bar.
- Mobile hamburger menu.
- Fully responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Navigation menu:
Home, About, Wedding Stories, Gallery, Films, Services, Packages, Contact

Overall goal:
Create a luxurious, emotional, conversion-focused Indian wedding photography website that makes couples feel the brand is premium, trustworthy, creative, and perfect for capturing their once-in-a-lifetime memories.
